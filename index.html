Welcome Home

Gameplay Programmer; 2D Asset Dev

Unity Engine, Adobe Photoshop

Welcome Home is a top-down single player arcade-style game created in a team of 3 as a submission for Technica 2021. You play as a teenage boy scrambling to clean the house after a raging party before your parents make it back home. I created the player sprite as well as the different cleaning supplies within the game. I implemented the functionality for cleaning. Within 24 hours, we developed the majority of the game. It is available to view on Itch.io.

Martial Lawless

2D Artist

Unity Engine, Piskel, Aseprite

C#

Martial Lawless is a 2D top-down beat 'em up style game based in Unity with a team of 6. You play as a vengeful martial artist being attacked for betraying his clan. I designed the main character, created two character animations, designed the environment the game takes place in, and created the main menu screen and game over screen. The game was well received during playtests and there is a build available to play!

Croak & Dagger

Level Designer

Unity Engine, Probuilder

C#

Croak & Dagger is a 3D single player hack 'n slash game created with a larger team as a personal project. You play as a grief-stricken frog fighting to avenge the loss of his children with the aid of a sole surviving egg that can transform into different weapons. As of writing this, I have been tasked with grayboxing the level leading up to the boss fight within the game. I have also contributed to the overall level design of the game. This game is currently in development. Though I am no longer on the development team, the GitHub page is being updated regularly.
